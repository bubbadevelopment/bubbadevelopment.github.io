🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Bubba's Golf Scorecard! Our app makes it easy to track your golf scores across all your devices with automatic iCloud sync.

Starting Your First Round

  1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  2. Enter the course name and number of holes (9 or 18)
  3. Add player names
  4. Tap "Start Round" to begin scoring

Entering Scores

Simply tap on a player's hole to enter their score. Your progress is automatically saved after each hole, so you'll never lose your data!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does iCloud sync work?

Your golf rounds are automatically synced across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Make sure you have iCloud enabled in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud. Changes typically sync within seconds when you have an internet connection.

What if I lose internet connection during a round?

No problem! The app works perfectly offline. All your scores are saved locally and will automatically sync to iCloud when you're back online.

Can I edit a score after entering it?

Yes! Just tap on any score in the scorecard to edit it. You can also view and edit past rounds from the History tab.

How do I resume an incomplete round?

If you leave a round before completing all holes, it's automatically saved. You can resume it anytime from the home screen where incomplete rounds are displayed.

Can I track multiple players in one round?

Absolutely! When setting up a new round, you can add up to 4 players. Each player's scores are tracked individually with automatic score-to-par calculations.

How do I delete a round?

In the History tab, swipe left on any round and tap "Delete". Note: Deleted rounds are removed from all your synced devices.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your data is stored in your private iCloud account with end-to-end encryption. We never see or have access to your golf scores or personal information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

What devices are supported?

Bubba's Golf Scorecard requires iOS 18.0 or later on iPhone, or iPadOS 18.0 or later on iPad. The app is optimized for both platforms with native interfaces.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Sync Issues

If your rounds aren't syncing between devices:

  • Check that you're signed in to iCloud on all devices
  • Verify you have an internet connection
  • Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and ensure iCloud Drive is enabled
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Check your iCloud storage - make sure you haven't run out of space

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app from the App Store
  • Try force-quitting the app and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, contact support below

Scores Not Saving

  • Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Check that you're running iOS 18.0 or later
  • Try force-quitting and reopening the app
